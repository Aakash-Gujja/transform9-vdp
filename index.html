Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

At Transform9, we take the security of our products and services seriously. We appreciate the efforts of security researchers who help us identify vulnerabilities responsibly. This policy explains how to report issues and what to expect.

Scope

This policy covers all publicly accessible systems and services operated by Transform9, including but not limited to:

  • https://www.transform9.com and its subpaths
  • https://portal.transform9.com and its subpaths
  • APIs under the *.transform9.com domain

Out of Scope

The following are not covered by this program:

  • Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
  • Social engineering such as phishing or scams
  • Physical attacks on hardware or facilities
  • Third-party services not owned by Transform9
  • UI/UX issues without security impact
  • Clickjacking or missing headers without exploitability

How to Submit a Vulnerability Report

Please send detailed reports to security@transform9.com with:

  • Description and reproduction steps
  • Impact assessment
  • Proof of concept (if possible)
  • Contact information for follow-up (optional)

Our Commitment to You

  • Acknowledge your report within 3 business days
  • Keep you updated on the investigation and resolution
  • Coordinate for responsible disclosure
  • Provide credit if you desire

Legal Safe Harbor

As long as you follow this policy and act in good faith, we commit not to pursue legal action related to your testing. Please avoid any activities that could harm users or systems.
